Our Mission Statement

We the people of God at St. Mary of the Lake Catholic Church believe Godâ€™s love touches all people through Jesus Christ. We believe the Catholic faith offers light against darkness and confusion and strength to triumph over the difficulties of life. We believe the Gospels challenge us to confront issues of poverty, violence, crime, racism, sexism, and fear.

Our Baptismal promises lead us to commit ourselves with the help of the Holy Spirit to evangelize:
As we worship God through Eucharist and Sacramental Liturgies;
As we strengthen the bonds of community life;
As we promote social justice and human dignity;
As we provide lifelong opportunities to learn and grow in our faith;
As we become good stewards of the blessings we have received;
We invite all people of good will to join us on this journey?
